My story

Hello, I'm Dmitry. I am a junior front-end developer from Russia. I started my journey in 2023, when I decided to change my life. I have always been interested in working with computers and learning new programs. Developing new products with my own hands, understanding programming languages, writing code and mastering new technologies is a fascinating process for me.

At the moment, I am self-studying all the material on front-end development. Services like roadmap.sh and The Odin Project are very helpful to me. I have already mastered several web technologies and have several projects on GitHub, each of which is a consolidation of new knowledge for me.

I really enjoy programming and I am happy to learn something new and improve my code every day. I clearly see my way forward - after front-end, I want to apply my skills in back-end, to become a full-stack developer. I know what technologies I have to master, and this motivates me.

To become better, I spend as much time as possible on developing my web skills and English language. In addition, I have a hobby related to computers - I have a home server Home Assistant on Raspberry Pi OS, on which I additionally practice my skills in working with Linux.
